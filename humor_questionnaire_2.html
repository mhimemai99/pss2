Sense of Humor Response Questionnaire

Based on Boyle & Joss-Reid (2004)

Instructions:

For each of the following situations, indicate how much you would typically respond with laughter or amusement by selecting a number from the scale below.

Response Scale:
1 = I would not find it funny at all
2 = I would smile
3 = I would chuckle
4 = I would laugh moderately
5 = I would laugh heartily
1. If you were telling a funny story to a group of friends and you accidentally messed up the punchline, how likely would you be to laugh at yourself?
2. If you slipped on a banana peel in front of a crowd, how much would you laugh instead of feeling embarrassed?
3. If you were watching a comedy movie alone, how likely would you be to laugh out loud?
4. If a friend made a clever joke at your expense, would you laugh along with them?
5. If you were in a tense situation (e.g., waiting for an important exam result), and someone made a lighthearted joke, how likely would you be to laugh?
6. If you saw a funny sign or an unusual typo in a public place, would you laugh or point it out to others?
7. If you were playfully teased by a close friend, how likely would you be to find it funny rather than feeling offended?
8. If something unexpected but harmless happened (e.g., a waiter accidentally bringing you the wrong order in a humorous way), would you laugh about it?
Please answer all questions before calculating your score.
